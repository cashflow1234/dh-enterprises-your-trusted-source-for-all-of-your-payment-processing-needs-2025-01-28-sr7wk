About DH Enterprises

Founded in 2009, DH Enterprises emerged from a vision to revolutionize payment processing for businesses of all sizes. Our journey began when our founders identified critical gaps in the payment industry - high fees, poor customer service, and complex integration processes. What started as a small team has grown into a trusted payment solutions provider serving thousands of businesses worldwide.

Our mission is to empower businesses with secure, efficient, and affordable payment solutions while maintaining the highest standards of customer service. We believe in transparency, innovation, and building lasting relationships with our clients. Our core values of integrity, excellence, and customer-first approach drive everything we do, from product development to support services.

Frequently Asked Questions

What security measures do you implement?

We employ bank-level encryption, tokenization, and AI-powered fraud detection. Our systems are PCI DSS Level 1 certified and regularly audited by third-party security firms.

What are your processing fees and rates?

We offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Rates start at 2.4% + $0.30 per transaction, with volume discounts available. Custom pricing available for high-volume merchants.

How long does integration take?

Standard integrations can be completed within 24-48 hours. Custom solutions typically take 3-5 business days. Our technical team provides support throughout the process.

What payment methods do you support?

We support all major credit cards, digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), ACH transfers, and various international payment methods across 150+ currencies.

What kind of support do you offer?

We provide 24/7 technical support, dedicated account managers, and comprehensive documentation. Emergency support is available within 15 minutes.
